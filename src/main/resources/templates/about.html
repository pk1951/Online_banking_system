About TRUE Bank

Meet the team behind TRUE Bank's innovative online banking system

TRUE Bank's online banking system is a comprehensive solution designed to provide secure, reliable, and innovative banking services to our customers. Our platform offers a wide range of features including account management, transaction processing, and loan applications, all within a user-friendly interface.

This project was developed by a talented team of software engineers from PES University EC Campus as part of their Object-Oriented Analysis and Design course.
